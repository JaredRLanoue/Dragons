Welcome to Groupify!

Our application is designed to help you find and connect with groups of likeminded individuals to accomplish whatever goal you have in mind. With our easy-to-use platform, you can create and post your own group or join an already posted group to do any number of activities. Examples include finding a few friends to play video games with or even to start a local event at a nearby park. The posible events or activities Groupify can help you with are limitless!
